Clifton Parents Requiring Action and Information for Special Education (P.R.A.I.S.E.) is a non-adversarial parent support group for parents and families with special needs children and adults. We have monthly group presentation meetings on the 4th Monday of every month at 7 p.m. during the school year. We have weekly group support meetings at 7 p.m. every Saturday. All are welcome to attend and join us.
