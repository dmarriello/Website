PRAISE Baseball was formed April 2017 as a Babe Ruth Bambino Buddy Ball League through Clifton Junior Athletic League for athletes aged 5-18. We strongly believe every child has the right to participate in the game of baseball and to be a part of a team. This division makes allowance for a “buddy” to help the differently abled athlete swing a bat, round the bases, catch a ball, etc. Our team allows our athletes to develop confidence and positive self-esteem. It allows them to be part of an organized sport and assists with physical fitness as well as the development of social skills. Our team is designed so that everyone has the opportunity to have fun and be accepted as they are.
